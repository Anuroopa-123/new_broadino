Broadino's is a parent company in India is Broadline Innovations Private limited is an ISO 9001:2008-certified company with over three decades of rich experience in Software development, System Integration, Application development and Best computing Practices. Broadline incorporated in 1991 based at Chennai , India provides a complete spectrum of consulting, technology, Hardware, Networking and product engineering services. In a crowded and intensely competitive marketplace for such offerings, we have built a reputation and core differentiating attribute around our unique Business Outcomes-based model. Armed with over two decades of IT Services experience and our distinctive philosophy of Accountable for Clients Business, our multi-location organization consistently delivers effective solutions to many Government, Public sector & Corporate clients.

Broadline has been instrumental in innovating many State and Federal Government IT initiatives for e-Governance, especially those centered on the Health, Defense, Education, Public Sector, and Power industries. In the Public Health sector, with a special focus on developing a comprehensive model, Broadline had designed and developed a system that was widely appreciated by the WHO and World Bank. The system was further scaled and adapted for use in implementing the prestigious Prime Minister’s Rural Health Mission for the rest of India, and was even extended to five other foreign countries. This initiative by Broadline won the prestigious Prime Minister’s E-Governance Award for successful innovation in the year 2000. Broadline has also been productively involved in pioneering and developing Power and Energy sector applications in the States of Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, and Madhya Pradesh. Additionally, Broadline has partnered with Siemens to guide and mentor over 100 thousand students, empowering the next generation of technology professionals with real-world knowledge and opportunities.
